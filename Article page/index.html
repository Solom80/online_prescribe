Are you curious about telehealth cost comparisons in this digital era? Telehealth and online doctor services in Australia have become crucial services that nobody would want to be without since the onset of COVID-19.

In fact, according to the Australian Digital Health Agency, from the first two weeks of the pandemic in mid-March 2020 through July 31, 2022, there have been more than 118.2 million telehealth consultations provided to over 18 million patients.

Up to 95,000 health professionals have adopted the use of telehealth in their practice. Furthermore, Australians have contributed an estimated $106 million to support the continuity of telehealth services.

If you're finding it difficult to fit telehealth services into your budget, you're not alone. In this article, we will reveal 10 of the most affordable online healthcare options in Australia and guide you through the process of securing affordable healthcare costs without sacrificing quality and accessibility.
